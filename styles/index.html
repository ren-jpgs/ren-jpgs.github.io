I love cheese even though I am slightly lactose intolerant

Here are my top 5 cheeses

(not in any specific order)



Parmesan

    Why I love Parmesan
    1. It has such a strong flavor and the more it is aged the better it gets.
    2. The cheese has a nutty taste that pairs well with pasta.
    3. Parmesan goes well with cured meats like prosciutto, soppressata, or mortadella and green grapes
    4. It has a gritty texture that feels like the equivalent of crushed peanuts in crunchy peanut butter
    5. I can basically snack on a whole block of Parmesan alone (sadly)

Pepper Jack

    Why I love Pepper Jack cheese
    1. Although it isn't such fancy cheese, it works so well in hardy comfort foods like a burger or sandwich
    2. Is not too overpowering, the flavors are subtle but are really great
    3. Pepper Jack gives a little kick in the foods you put it in and can enhance the dish because of the little spices in the cheese.
    4. Add some to your mac and cheese OR breakfast omelette and you will transcend
